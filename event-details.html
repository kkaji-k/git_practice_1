Summer Rock Festival

April 15, 2025
18:00 - 23:00
Riverside Arena, 123 River St, City
Organizer: Rock Produ ctions
Ticket Information

Event Description

Join us for the biggest rock festival of the summer! The Summer Rock Festival brings together the best rock bands for an unforgettable evening of music, energy, and fun.

Lineup

  • The Midnight Rocks (Headliner) - 21:00
  • Electric Tigers - 19:30
  • Stone Rebels - 18:00

What to Expect

Five hours of non-stop rock music from the best bands in the country. Food stalls and bars will be available throughout the venue. Merchandise will be available for purchase.

Important Information

  • Gates open at 17:00
  • No re-entry once you leave the venue
  • No outside food or drinks allowed
  • The event will take place rain or shine
  • All ages welcome, but those under 16 must be accompanied by an adult
